## RD3-A. VANITY (cabinet + top + sink + basic faucet, installed)

- RD3-A1. **Material — cabinet:** stock 36–48 inch **$200–$800** · semi-custom **$800–$3,500** · custom
  **$3,000–$10,000+**. *Driver:* particleboard vs plywood box vs solid-wood face-frame vs full inset.
- RD3-A2. **Countertop (per sq ft):** laminate $10–$40 · cultured marble ~$20–$40 · quartz $50–$200 ·
  granite $5–$100 · Corian ~$20–$75. **Quartz = most durable 2026 (engineered, non-porous).**
- RD3-A3. **Install labor:** basic swap $150–$700 (3–6 hrs) · full replacement $665–$3,300. Plumber
  $45–$120/hr for water/drain work.
- RD3-A4. **Installed tiers (single 36–48 inch):** budget ~$300–$800 · mid ~$1,200–$2,600 (national avg ~$1,500)
  · premium/custom $3,000–$8,000+ (double-sink, floating, stone top).
- RD3-A5. **Floating vanity:** cabinet priced like floor-mount; ADD reinforced blocking — $50–$150 if walls
  open (gut), $200–$600 retrofit into finished wall. (Ties SI-7 backing note.)
- RD3-A6. Hidden add-ons often missed: old-vanity removal $115–$500; backsplash repair $200–$600; paint
  touch-up $50–$200; hardware $30–$150.

## RD3-B. TOILET

- RD3-B1. **Material:** budget $100–$300 · mid $300–$500 · premium $500–$800+ · smart/bidet $1,500–$5,000.
- RD3-B2. **Install labor:** ~$375 avg (national). Like-for-like = Tier-3; relocating the drain = Tier-2 +
  plumbing (RD3-F). *Note SB 407 (RD2-D2): a permit may force low-flow replacement house-wide in CA.*

## RD3-C. TILE (floor + wall/shower surround)

- RD3-C1. **Material (per sq ft):** ceramic/porcelain $1–$15 · natural stone (marble) $5–$50 · glass/mosaic
  accent higher. **Porcelain = 2026 mid default (durable, water-resistant); stable pricing.**
- RD3-C2. **Install labor (per sq ft):** floor tile **$16.58–$20.60** (Homewyse national avg) · general
  $5–$20 · specialist $12–$22. Small/mosaic/patterned tile costs more (more cuts, slower set).
- RD3-C3. **Shower wall tile:** $7–$25/sq ft material. *Allowance candidate (SI-16) — wild variability.*
- RD3-C4. Waterproofing (under tile): sheet membrane (Schluter KERDI, Wedi) $3–$8/sq ft material, cuts labor
  30–40% (saves $500–$1,500/shower); liquid-applied (RedGard, Hydroban) $1.50–$3/sq ft, DIY-able. (Ties
  RD1-E; Tier-3 DIY-feasible.)

## RD3-D. SHOWER / TUB

- RD3-D1. **Prefab shower kit:** $300–$3,000 installed ($1,000–$3,000 typical w/ install).
- RD3-D2. **Custom tile shower:** starts ~$3,500, to $10,000+ (glass, bench, body jets). Install up to a week.
- RD3-D3. **Tub:** standard alcove $400–$2,000 · walk-in/jetted $2,000–$10,000 · freestanding (see RD1-F
  structural gate — filled weight can trip Tier-1 review).
- RD3-D4. **Shower door:** bypass (light) low-cost; frameless heavy glass swings budget thousands (long-lead —
  RD2-F bottleneck).

## RD3-E. FIXTURES + LIGHTING + VENT

- RD3-E1. **Sink:** $40–$300 (+~$400 install). **Faucet:** $70–$900 (wall-mount higher). **Showerhead:**
  $50–$600 basic, to $2,000 designer.
- RD3-E2. **Lighting:** fixtures $200–$700 typical; total lighting upgrade $150–$2,000 (rewire adds). Install
  usually a few hours for fixture swap; new circuit = Tier-2/electrician (RD1-A). *CA Title 24: high-efficacy
  LED mandatory when replacing >50% fixtures (RD2-D1).* IES targets in RD-4.
- RD3-E3. **Exhaust fan:** ~$396 avg install ($115–$900 range); new ductwork $250–$950. Min 50 CFM
  intermittent / 20 continuous, vented outdoors (RD1-D). *CA Title 24 may require HRV (RD2-D1).*

## RD3-F. PLUMBING / ELECTRICAL LABOR (the relocation cost drivers)

- RD3-F1. **Plumbing rough-in / relocation:** $400–$2,200 (avg ~$1,500) for moving a fixture; full bath
  rough-in $3,000–$8,000 (to $20k for extensive). PEX $1.50–$4/lin ft · copper $4–$12/lin ft. **Keeping
  fixtures in place is the single biggest cost-saver** — relocation adds labor + permit + inspection (Tier-2).
- RD3-F2. **Electrical:** $800–$2,500 typical mid-remodel; each new outlet/fixture $100–$300 labor; new
  circuit/panel = Tier-1 (RD1-A/permit).
- RD3-F3. **Demolition + haul:** $1,000–$2,300 national (RD2-C2: San Jose $1,500–$2,500).

## RD3-G. INSTALL TIMELINES  [industry-average + best-case, per prefs]

- RD3-G1. Vanity swap (like-for-like): **avg 3–6 hrs; best-case ~2 hrs** (stock, integrated top, same
  plumbing). Custom w/ plumbing moves: 2–3 days.
- RD3-G2. Tile shower + surround: **avg 3–5 days** (set + cure + grout); best-case ~2 days (small, membrane
  system). Custom tile shower up to a week.
- RD3-G3. Toilet/faucet/fixture swap: **avg a few hours each; best-case <1 hr** like-for-like.
- RD3-G4. Full itemized install stacks into RD-2 whole-project timeline (4–8 wk avg / 2–3 wk best-case) —
  sequencing (plumbing→tile, electrical→drywall) is the gate, not any single item (RD2-F3).

## RD3-H. ALLOWANCE ITEMS  [wild-variability finishes → SI-16 allowance mode]

- RD3-H1. Flag as allowance (user sets unit cost WITH basis, code-validated SI-16): tile (floor/wall/mosaic),
  countertop stone, faucets/showerheads, shower glass, decorative lighting. These swing most on taste/brand.
- RD3-H2. Banded (from this table): standard toilet, exhaust fan, basic sink, prefab shower, plumbing/
  electrical labor, demo/haul.
