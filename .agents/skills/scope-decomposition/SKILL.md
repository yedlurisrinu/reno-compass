# Scope Decomposition

Turn an open-ended renovation goal into structured, gate-ready scope. This is judgment work; the elicitation
wording lives in the scope workflow. Governed by input-is-evidence (SI-3) and sensitive-data handling (SI-6).

## What to produce
- `goal` + resale motive if stated; `property_context` (dwelling_type, home_age, zipcode, occupant ages).
- `must_have` vs `nice_to_have` priorities — the fidelity line Design is held to (scope-creep guard).
- `special_considerations` (accessibility, health, allergies, pets) — sensitive, optional, ask-once
  (see sensitive-data rule). Allergies is the safety-critical exception.
- `intended_timing`, budget target/ceiling.

## How to reason
- Treat what the family says as evidence, not ground truth — validate, enrich, flag implausible inputs.
- Separate what they want (goal) from what they assume (solution) — capture the goal, let the pipeline design.
- Surface hidden-condition likelihoods early via home_age (hand off to hidden-condition-surfacing).
- Do not price here beyond the ballpark horizon; the reality-check threshold decides plausible/tight/unrealistic
  (pricing-ballpark skill, RD2-G).

## Boundaries
- No safety classification here (that is Safety's sole authority).
- Ask sensitive topics once; never re-ask a declined field; never infer medical conditions unstated.
