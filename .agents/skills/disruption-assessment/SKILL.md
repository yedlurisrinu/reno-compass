# Disruption Assessment

Determine the disruption footprint so the family knows what they're living through.

## What to produce
`disruption` — offline utilities, offline duration estimate. Feed into the live-through-it determination.

## How to reason
- Duration reasons on trade sequencing and timing; give industry-average + best-case.
- A single-bathroom home with the only bath offline is high-impact — surface it plainly.
- Timelines are ranges; ordering materials before demo is the biggest on-schedule lever.
