# Phase Checklist

Produce execution checklists — but ONLY when the family accepted a design (`design_accepted == true`).
Checklists are execution artifacts; a rejected-all outcome (with or without a budget gap) gets no checklist.

## Sections
- before_demolition (order materials first — the biggest on-schedule lever; confirm permits filed)
- after_demolition (inspect for hidden conditions before closing walls)
- while_reno_in_progress (inspection milestones; keep waterproofing before tile)
- wrap_up (final inspection sign-off before final payment)

## Framing
Timelines industry-average + best-case. Permitting adds ~1–3 weeks (to 3–5 in strict metros; best case ~1 week
simple scope). Custom vanities / specialty tile / frameless glass carry 2–4 week lead times.
